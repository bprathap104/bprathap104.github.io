Medical Specialists

A list of various medical specialists and their roles.

Endocrinologist

An endocrinologist specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of hormone-related diseases and conditions.

Neurologist

A neurologist deals with disorders of the nervous system, including the brain and spinal cord.

Oncologist

An oncologist is a doctor who specializes in diagnosing and treating cancer.

Cardiologist

A cardiologist specializes in diagnosing and treating diseases of the cardiovascular system.

Gynecologist

A gynecologist focuses on women's reproductive health.

Psychiatrist

A psychiatrist specializes in diagnosing, treating, and preventing mental health disorders.

General Practitioner

A general practitioner provides primary and continuing care to patients of all ages.

Pediatrician

A pediatrician manages the health of children, including physical, behavior, and mental health issues.

Radiologist

A radiologist uses medical imaging to diagnose and treat diseases and injuries.

Dermatologist

A dermatologist specializes in treating skin, hair, and nail conditions.

Gastroenterologist

A gastroenterologist treats conditions related to the digestive system.

Nephrologist

A nephrologist specializes in kidney care and treating diseases of the kidneys.

Allergist

An allergist diagnoses and treats allergic conditions.

Emergency Medicine

Emergency medicine specialists provide immediate decision making and action to prevent death or any further disability.

Hematologist

A hematologist specializes in diseases of the blood and blood components.

Orthopaedist

An orthopaedist focuses on the diagnosis, correction, prevention, and treatment of skeletal deformities.

Pulmonologist

A pulmonologist specializes in the respiratory system.

Surgeon

A surgeon performs operations to treat or prevent diseases, repair damage, or improve the body function or appearance.

Ophthalmologist

An ophthalmologist specializes in eye and vision care.

Geriatrician

A geriatrician focuses on the health care of elderly people.

Infectious Disease Specialist

An infectious disease specialist deals with complex infections.

Podiatrist

A podiatrist specializes in diagnosing and treating conditions of the foot, ankle, and related structures of the leg.

Dentist

A dentist specializes in oral health and treats conditions affecting the teeth and gums.

[O

Internal Medicine

Internal medicine specialists, or internists, diagnose and treat adult diseases.
